Recommendation for Recurring Transactions

For your recurring transaction form, I'd suggest combining approaches:

  • Option 3 (Quick Chips) for the "Next Date" field - fast selection of common dates
  • Option 5 (Relative Dates) integrated with frequency - when frequency is "monthly", show day-of-month selector
  • Option 4 (Bottom Sheet) as fallback for custom date selection
