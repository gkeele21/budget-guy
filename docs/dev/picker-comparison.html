Observations with 20 Categories

Bottom Sheet: Works reasonably well - the grouped headers help organize, and scrolling feels natural. The partial coverage keeps context of where you are.

Dropdown: Gets cramped quickly. The small scrollable area makes it hard to browse, especially on mobile where touch targets feel tight.

Full Modal: The search box becomes very useful with this many options. If users know what they want, they can type "groc" and jump right to Groceries. The full screen feels more intentional.
